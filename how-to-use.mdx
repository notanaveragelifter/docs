## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before using PNP Exchange, ensure you have:
- A Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, or similar)
- USDC tokens for market creation and trading
- Basic understanding of prediction markets

### Connecting Your Wallet

1. Visit the PNP Exchange platform
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top navigation
3. Select your preferred Solana wallet
4. Approve the connection request

## Market Creation

### General Prediction Markets

Create markets for events across sports, politics, technology, cryptocurrency, and culture.

**Settlement Method**: LLM-assisted oracles (Perplexity + Grok)

### Token Price Prediction Markets

Create markets for price predictions of any Solana-based token, including newly launched tokens.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/SS6.png" alt="Token prediction market creation interface" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />

4. Set initial liquidity
5. Submit for market creation

## Market Settlement Analysis

After market creation, the oracle system performs initial analysis to determine market resolvability.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/SS7.png" alt="Market settlement analysis in progress" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**Analysis Process:**
1. Oracle evaluates market question for resolvability
2. Identifies authoritative data sources
3. Generates settlement criteria
4. Markets are marked as "Resolvable" or "Non-resolvable"

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/SS8.png" alt="Market resolvability determination" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**Market States:**
- **Resolvable**: Market can be settled using available data sources
- **Non-resolvable**: Market cannot be objectively settled, eligible for creator refund

1. Select the outcome you want to bet on (YES or NO)

**Trading Features:**
- Real-time price updates
- Instant liquidity through bonding curves
- Transparent fee structure (1% trading fee)
- No slippage from order book mechanics

## Fee Management

### Claiming Creator Fees

Market creators can claim accumulated fees from their markets at any time.

<Frame>
  <img src="/claimfees.png" alt="Creator fees interface with claim button" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**Fee Claiming:**
1. Navigate to your profile dashboard
2. View accumulated fees across all your markets
3. Click "Claim Fees" to withdraw all accumulated fees
4. Fees are automatically distributed to your wallet

**Fee Structure:**
- 50% of all trading fees go to market creators
- Fees accumulate automatically as markets are traded
- No minimum threshold required for claiming

### Refunding Non-resolvable Markets

If a market is marked as non-resolvable, creators can reclaim their initial liquidity.

<Frame>
  <img src="/refund.png" alt="Non-resolvable market refund interface" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

**Refund Process:**
1. Go to your profile's "My Markets" section
2. Find markets marked as "Non-resolvable"
3. Click "Claim Refund" on the specific market
4. Initial liquidity is returned to your wallet

**Refund Eligibility:**
- Market must be marked as non-resolvable by the oracle
- Only the market creator can claim the refund
- Refund amount equals the initial liquidity provided

**Dashboard Features:**

**Market Status Tracking:**
- **Active**: Market is currently accepting trades

- **Resolved**: Market has been settled with a winning outcome
- **Non-resolvable**: Market cannot be settled, eligible for refund

## Best Practices

### Market Creation
- Create clear, specific questions that can be objectively answered
- Set realistic end times that allow for sufficient trading activity
- Provide adequate initial liquidity to ensure market viability
- Choose appropriate market types based on available data sources

### Trading
- Research markets thoroughly before placing trades
- Consider the time remaining and settlement criteria
- Diversify your positions across multiple markets
- Monitor your portfolio regularly for market updates

### Risk Management
- Only trade with funds you can afford to lose
- Understand that prediction markets involve inherent risk
- Review settlement criteria before placing trades
- Keep track of your positions and market statuses
