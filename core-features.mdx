## Protocol Features

### Market Creation

**Permissionless Market Deployment**
- No approval process or gatekeepers required
- Instant market creation with Solana wallet and USDC collateral
- Configurable parameters: question, end time, initial liquidity
- Markets become tradeable immediately upon creation

**Supported Market Types**
- Token price predictions (any Solana-based token)
- Social media events and announcements
- Sports outcomes and political events
- Custom questions with verifiable resolution sources

### Bonding Curve AMM

**Pythagorean Bonding Curve Implementation**
- Continuous pricing mechanism without order books
- Automatic price discovery based on supply and demand
- Guaranteed liquidity from first trade
- Mathematical formula: `r² = s_yes² + s_no² + s_draw²`

**Key Properties**
- Predictable pricing mechanics
- No liquidity provider risk
- Works for long-tail and niche markets
- Transparent and auditable price calculations

### Oracle Settlement System

**LLM-Assisted Oracle Architecture**
- Structured evidence evaluation using large language models
- Time-bounded data collection to prevent hindsight bias
- Programmatic evidence collection and auditability
- Deterministic YES/NO outcomes with transparent reasoning

**Settlement Process**
1. Criteria generation and resolvability assessment
2. Evidence collection from authoritative sources
3. LLM evaluation with structured prompts
4. On-chain settlement with verifiable outcomes

### Fee Structure

**Trading Fee Distribution (1% total)**
- 50% → Market Creators (incentivizes quality market creation)
- 15% → Platform Operations (development and infrastructure)
- 15% → $PNP Token Holders (staking rewards and buybacks)
- 10% → Referral Program (user acquisition incentives)
- 10% → Insurance Fund (protocol security and risk management)

**Fee Mechanics**
- Applied to all buy and sell transactions
- Transparent on-chain accrual and distribution
- Creator fees claimable at any time
- Automatic fee collection and redistribution

### Token Economics

**Fair Launch Model**
- 100% community distribution at launch
- 0% team allocation or private sales
- 0% reserved tokens
- True community ownership from day one

**Token Utility**
- Fee sharing for token holders (15% of trading fees)
- Governance rights for protocol decisions
- Enhanced benefits for staked tokens
- Twitter-native market creation capabilities

### Technical Architecture

**On-Chain Components**
- Solana program for market operations
- SPL Token integration for outcome tokens
- USDC as collateral token
- Program-derived addresses for market accounts

**Integration Points**
- Composable with existing DeFi infrastructure
- Open protocol for third-party integrations
- SDK for developer integration
- API endpoints for market data access
