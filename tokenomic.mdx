## Token Distribution Model

### Fair Launch Implementation

PNP Exchange implemented a fair launch token distribution model with complete community ownership from day one. The token was launched through Believe.app, a platform that enables fair token distribution without presales or team allocations.

**Distribution Breakdown:**
- **100% Community Distribution** - All tokens available to the public at launch
- **0% Team Allocation** - No tokens reserved for founders or team members
- **0% Private Sales** - No presales or early investor advantages
- **0% Reserved Tokens** - Complete supply distributed through open market participation

## Token Utility Mechanisms

### Revenue Sharing Model

The $PNP token serves as the primary mechanism for protocol value accrual through fee distribution:

**Fee Distribution (1% total trading fees):**
- **50%** → Market Creators (incentivizes quality market creation)
- **15%** → $PNP Token Holders (proportional distribution)
- **15%** → Platform Operations (development and infrastructure)
- **10%** → Referral Program (user acquisition incentives)
- **10%** → Insurance Fund (protocol security and risk management)

## Economic Model

### Value Accrual Mechanisms

**Primary Revenue Streams:**
- Trading fees from prediction market volume
- Fee sharing creates direct token value accrual
- Platform growth directly benefits token holders

**Value Drivers:**
- **Trading Volume**: Increased volume = higher fee distributions
- **Platform Adoption**: More users = greater token utility
- **Governance Participation**: Decentralized control through token voting
- **Fair Distribution**: Sustainable tokenomics through community ownership

### Sustainability Design

The tokenomics model ensures long-term sustainability through:
- **Community Ownership**: 100% fair distribution prevents centralization
- **Value Alignment**: Token holders benefit from protocol success
- **Transparent Distribution**: All fees and distributions are on-chain and verifiable
- **Incentive Structure**: Rewards for market creators, traders, and long-term holders

### Economic Parameters

**Fee Structure:**
- **Trading Fee**: 1% on all buy/sell transactions
- **Distribution**: Automatic on-chain distribution to designated recipients
- **Claiming**: Creator fees claimable at any time
- **Transparency**: All distributions publicly verifiable on-chain
