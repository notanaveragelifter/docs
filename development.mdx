## Fee Overview

PNP Exchange implements a transparent 1% trading fee on all market transactions. This fee is automatically collected and distributed across protocol participants according to predefined allocation percentages, ensuring sustainable protocol economics and community value alignment.

### Fee Collection

**Trading Fee Rate**: 1% on all buy and sell transactions
**Collection Method**: Automatic deduction during token trades
**Transparency**: All fee collection and distribution is on-chain and verifiable

## Fee Distribution Model

### Allocation Breakdown

The 1% trading fee is distributed across five categories:

**Market Creators (50%)**
- Direct reward for creating valuable prediction markets
- Claimable on-chain at any time by market creators
- Incentivizes high-quality market creation and curation
- Proportional to trading volume generated by each market

**Platform Operations (15%)**
- Funds ongoing development and infrastructure maintenance
- Supports protocol upgrades and feature development
- Ensures long-term platform sustainability and growth

**$PNP Token Holders (15%)**
- Distributed proportionally to token holders
- Creates passive income for long-term token holders
- Aligns token holder incentives with protocol success
- Supports token value accrual through fee distribution

**Referral Program (10%)**
- Incentivizes platform growth and user acquisition
- Rewards users who bring new participants to the protocol
- Supports organic growth and community expansion

**Insurance Fund (10%)**
- Provides protocol security and risk management
- Covers potential edge cases and emergency scenarios
- Ensures protocol stability and user protection

## Economic Rationale

### Value Creation Alignment

**Market Creator Incentives**
- 50% allocation recognizes market creators as primary value drivers
- Direct correlation between market quality and creator rewards
- Encourages creation of high-engagement, valuable markets

**Community Ownership**
- 65% of fees (50% creators + 15% token holders) flow to community participants
- Transparent distribution prevents value extraction by protocol operators
- Aligns all participants with protocol success and growth

**Sustainable Operations**
- 35% of fees fund essential operations and growth initiatives
- Balanced allocation ensures protocol sustainability
- Supports long-term development and infrastructure needs

## Technical Implementation

### On-Chain Distribution

**Automatic Collection**
- Fees are collected automatically during each trade
- No manual intervention required for fee collection
- Transparent and verifiable on-chain transactions

**Distribution Mechanism**
- Fees are distributed according to predefined percentages
- Market creator fees are claimable through smart contract functions
- Token holder distributions occur automatically based on holdings

**Transparency Features**
- All fee collection and distribution is publicly verifiable
- Complete audit trail of all fee transactions
- Real-time tracking of fee accumulation and distribution

### Claiming Process

**Market Creators**
- Creator fees accumulate automatically as markets are traded
- Claimable at any time through the protocol interface
- No minimum threshold or waiting period required

**Token Holders**
- Fee distributions occur automatically based on token holdings
- Proportional to individual token holder balances
- Integrated with staking mechanisms for enhanced rewards
